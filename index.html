About me

Design,
for me,
is intention.

I'm a UI/UX and Product Designer based in India, with a deep curiosity for how people interact with the digital world. I care about understanding people before designing for them.

My work spans mobile apps, web products, and end-to-end design systems. Whether I'm mapping a user journey or refining a micro-interaction, I bring the same obsessive attention to detail to everything.

Outside of design, I'm a writer — and I find storytelling makes me a better designer. Both are about clarity, empathy, and saying exactly what needs to be said.
